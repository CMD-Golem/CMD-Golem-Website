Powered Enchanting

1.15 +

This Datapack changes the entire Enchanting System of Minecraft. In addition, there are a bunch of Addons with more custom Enchantments, which you can put together according to your needs.

In total there are three different Datapack Editions.

  • The CMD-Golem Edition, which adds 17 new Enchantments.
  • The Vanilla Edition, which only contains the Vanilla Minecraft Enchantments.
  • And the Empty Edition, which doesn't contain any Enchantments.

To each Edition you can install Addons according to your needs.

Get more Information on the Powered Enchanting Website

Enchanting

Build the Enchanting Table
Place the Enchanting Table and drop a Glowstone Dust on top of it, to show where you need to place the Pillars.
At the blue Particles you need to place two Lapis Lazuli Blocks on top of each other and at the white Particles you need to place one Chiseled Quartz Blocks.

Enchant Books
Drop a Book on top of the Enchanting Table. Then four Books will spawn and will move towards the Chiseled Quartz Blocks. After they reached the Blocks they will get the Name of an Enchantment. You can select one by placing a Lapis Lazuli Slice on top of Chiseled Quartz Block underneath.

Combining
To get Enchantment on a Tool or Armor you need to drop it together with the Enchanted Book or another Enchanted Item on top of an Enchanting Table. If you combine two items, the first one you drop will stay and get the Enchantments of the second.

Additional Enchantments

Auto Smelt

Smelts the Blocks you mine.

3x3 Mining

Mines a three by three area.

Freeze

Slows you target.

Leaping

Increases jump strength.

Life Steal

Per critical hit you get two absorption hearts until you have 20 Hearts.

Magma Walker

Turns lava near the player into cobblestone.

Magnetic

Attracts all items in the vicinity.

Nutrition

Feeds you automatically.

Perish

Gives the target the wither effect.

Reaping

Replants seeds.

Soft Falling

No fall damage.

Soulbound

Get items back after death.

Swiftness

Increases walking speed.

Timber

Breaks logs which are connected to the broken block.

Vein Miner

Breaks ores which are connected to the broken ore.

Venomous

Gives the target the poison effect.

Vision

Gives the player night vision.

Redstone Dot

1.16.x

This Data Pack won't be continued due to it is now possible in Minecraft to change the look of Redstone back to a dot when right clicking it.


Get the old look of a single Redstone, without the need of a Resource Pack.

If you break a Redstone in Creative mode, the other Redstones around it will not update, due to a limitation of Minecraft. In Survival mode this works without problems.

Sometimes it is also possible that a Redstone is not converted correctly.This can happen if the player moves fast during placement or if the Redstone is placed while the player looks very slightly past to a hitbox of a block.

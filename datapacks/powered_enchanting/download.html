Powered Enchanting

  1. Select the Minecraft Version you play in:
  2. Select a premade edition and/or select the enchantments you want in the list below.
    (If you don't select a vanilla enchantment, it will still be aviable trough trading or can be found in chests. It is also possible to combined it on the Enchanting Table.)
  3. Change values of each enchantment by clicking on the settings icon in the top-left corner.
  4. Download the Data Pack and enjoy your new enchantments.
    Use the Share button to share your create pack.
